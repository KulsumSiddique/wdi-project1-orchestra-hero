The Royal Albert Hall is located in South London and was opened by Queen Victoria in 1871. It's most famous for holding the Proms concerts every year since 1941.

Teatro alla Scala (La Scala) is an opera house in Milan, inaugurated in 1778. Some of the most revered opera composers from Puccini to Verdi have premiered their greatest works here.
